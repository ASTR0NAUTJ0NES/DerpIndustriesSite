About Us

We are Derp Industries! We first opened our
doors in 1997. We have had many breakthroughs in the
boxing industry and hope to accomplish many more. Boxing is our
number one priority. We package and ship boxes not only all over
the United States but we also ship internationally. We try
to remain diverse and optimistic within our company
and we like to pride ourselves on our
ability to think outside the "box."
